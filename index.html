Welcome to SOCF CrossFit
Our Classes
CrossFit

CrossFit is a fitness regimen developed by Greg Glassman over several decades. Glassman, CrossFit's Founder and CEO, was the first person in history to define fitness in a meaningful, measurable way.

Barbell Club

An olympic program designed around the use of the barbell. In the program, the athlete works with percentages in order to attempt and increase a maximum-weight single lift of a barbell.

Fit Kids

SOCF Fit Kids is a program that teaches children ages 7-12 different movements in order to keep them healthy and active. The program is based on the principle of mechanics, consistency, and intensity.

Bootcamp

Bootcamp classes are designed to give you a total body workout and improve your overall fitness level in the shortest time slot possible.

Adaptive Training

Participants in wheelchairs say the routines give them the strength to better perform everyday tasks as well as boosting theirself-esteem.CrossFit classes of the future could be more inclusive, thanks to a new pilot program at Orlando Health.

Personal Training

Personal Training classes are designed to give you a total body workout and improve your overall fitness level in a private setting.
